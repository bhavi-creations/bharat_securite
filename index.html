What We Offer

Our Security Services

Comprehensive protection solutions tailored to every environment and threat level — with clearly defined service types for every need.

01
Corporate Security

End-to-end protection for offices, IT parks, and commercial establishments with disciplined access control and visitor management systems.

Available Types
Reception & Access Control

Guard-managed entry points, visitor logs, ID verification and badge issuance.

Floor Security

Patrolling guards assigned per floor to deter internal threats and monitor movement.

After-Hours Watchman

Overnight static or roving guards to protect empty premises and assets.

02
Residential Security

24/7 protection for apartments, gated communities, and independent villas — ensuring complete peace of mind for every resident.

Available Types
Gated Community Guards

Boom-barrier management, resident vs. visitor verification and register maintenance.

Apartment Block Security

Lobby guards, intercom assistance and common area patrols for multi-storey buildings.

Villa / Bungalow Protection

Dedicated personal guards for individual residences with perimeter watch duties.

03
Industrial Security

Specialized deployment for factories, warehouses, and manufacturing units combining perimeter patrol with integrated CCTV oversight.

Available Types
Perimeter Patrol

Round-the-clock walking or vehicle patrols along boundary walls and fencing.

Material Movement Control

Gate checking of inbound and outbound vehicles, goods inspection and documentation.

Fire Safety Guard

Guards trained in industrial fire protocols, evacuation coordination and first response.

04
Personal Bodyguard

Highly trained personal protection officers for VIPs, corporate executives, and high-net-worth individuals requiring close-protection.

Available Types
Close Protection Officer (CPO)

Trained, armed or unarmed escorts accompanying the principal at all times.

Advance Security Detail

Pre-arrival venue checks, route planning and threat assessment for travel.

Female Protection Officer

Lady CPOs for female principals requiring discreet, same-gender protection.

05
Event Security

Professional crowd management and access control for corporate events, concerts, exhibitions, and private high-profile functions.

Available Types
Entry & Ticket Control

Bag screening, ticket verification and orderly queue management at all entry gates.

Crowd Control Team

Plainclothes and uniformed officers managing crowd density and preventing stampedes.

VIP Zone Management

Dedicated detail securing backstage, VVIP areas and green rooms throughout the event.

06
CCTV & Surveillance

Advanced remote monitoring solutions with real-time alerts, intelligent analytics, and professional ground-response integration.

Available Types
CCTV Installation & AMC

Supply, installation and annual maintenance of HD / PTZ camera networks.

Remote Video Monitoring

24/7 off-site monitoring with instant alerts to site guards and management.

AI Video Analytics

Motion detection, face recognition, loitering alerts and crowd heat-map reporting.
